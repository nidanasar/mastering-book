## Part 4 Integration Challenge (50 XP)

**The Master Influencer Challenge**

**Task**: Demonstrate mastery across all 30 principles in one comprehensive situation.

**Steps**:
1. Choose a high-stakes situation (family conflict, career negotiation, relationship repair)
2. Plan your approach using principles from all 4 parts
3. Execute with intentionality
4. Document the process and outcome

**Success Criteria**:
- ✅ Applied foundation principles (no criticism, appreciation, WIIFM)
- ✅ Used connection principles (interest, listening, importance)
- ✅ Demonstrated leadership principles (respect, sympathy, encouragement)
- ✅ Integrated mastery principles (face-saving, reputation, win-win)
- ✅ Achieved a positive outcome for all parties
